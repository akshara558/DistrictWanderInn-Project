Payyambalam Beach

Payyambalam Beach is a beautiful and serene beach located in Kannur, Kerala, India. It is known for its golden sands, clear blue waters, and tranquil atmosphere.

The beach is a popular spot for both locals and tourists, offering a perfect retreat for those seeking a peaceful escape. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll along the shoreline, take in the breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea, or simply relax and unwind.

Payyambalam Beach is also known for its well-maintained gardens and the iconic sculpture of Mother and Child designed by renowned sculptor Kanayi Kunhiraman. The sculpture adds to the artistic and cultural appeal of the beach.

Whether you're looking for a quiet place to enjoy the sunset or a picturesque spot for photography, Payyambalam Beach is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and beach enthusiasts.
